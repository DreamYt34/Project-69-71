Bai Tong

I had visited this place at july 24 2018

IT's a good place, My first grade teacher actually worked there part-time and liked to restaurant. The reason why I like this place so much is because of the quality of the food these days. Normally other chinese restaurants would have normal plates stuff like that, but bai tong actually changes your entire view of dining. The waiters have cool clothes and sporty shoes.
